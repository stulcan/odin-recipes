Bruschetta

Description

This tasty bruschetta recipe is sure to delight! Easy to make and easier to eat, bruschetta is a staple of Italian appetizers. Make all your friends jealous with this recipe that will be the talk of your potluck for years to come!

Ingredients

  • 4 slices Italian bread, 1/2 inch thick
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon garlic salt
  • 1 large tomato, sliced
  • 1 cup shredded Italian cheese blend

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven's broiler.
  2. Arrange the slices of bread in a single layer on a baking sheet.
  3. Mix together the olive oil and garlic powder in a cup or small bowl.
  4. Brush onto the slices of bread.
  5. Cover each piece of bread with tomato slices, then sprinkle with as much cheese as you like.
  6. Drizzle just a bit of the olive oil over the cheese.
  7. Broil for 3 ot 5 minutes, until cheese is melted and bread is toasty.
  8. Enjoy!
