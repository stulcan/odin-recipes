Tiramisu

Description

Tiramisu is a great dessert worth trying out yourself! This is a easy to make, family favorite recipe. The most important thing to remember is to use mascarpone and good quality coffee. These are the ingredients that will make your tiramisu stand above all the others!

Ingredients

  • 3 egg yolks
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 1/8 cups mascarpone cheese
  • 24 ladyfingers
  • 1 1/2 cups brewed coffee
  • 1 tablespoon unsweetened cocoa powder

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, beat yolks with sugar and vanilla until smooth and light yellow.
  2. Fold mascarpone into yolk mixture. Set aside.
  3. Dip ladyfingers briefly in coffee and arrange 12 of them in the bottom of an 8x8 inch dish.
  4. Spread half the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers.
  5. Repeat with remaining cookies and mascarpone.
  6. Cover and chill for 1 hour.
  7. Sprinkle with cocoa just before serving.
  8. Enjoy!
