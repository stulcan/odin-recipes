Decorated Sugar Cookies

Description

Learn how to bake and decorate your cookies all in one place! When regular sugar cookies aren't enough, adding royal icing to customise them is sure to get the job done.

Sugar Cookie Ingredients

  • 2 cups room temperature unsalted butter
  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 2 large room temperature eggs
  • 3 tablespoon real vanilla extract
  • 5 cups flour
  • 2 tablespoon salt
  • * Royal Icing *

Sugar Cookie Steps

  1. Cream butter and sugar together
  2. Add eggs, vanilla and salt
  3. Beat on high for 3-4 minutes
  4. Add flour 1 cup at a time
  5. When fully mixed, transfer to ziplock bag and refrigerate for at least 2 hours
  6. Roll out dough 1/4 inch thick on a well floured surface
  7. Cut out with cookie cutters
  8. Place cookies on parchment paper covered cookie sheet (aluminum, not no stick) and transfer to freezer for 10 minutes
  9. Bake cookies directly from freezer for 12-14 minutes, or until golden brown
  10. Decorate with royal icing when cooled

Royal Icing Ingredients

  • 3 oz egg whites (must be from real eggs, not cartons)
  • 4 cups powdered sugar (mare as desired)
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla (optional)

Royal Icing Steps

  1. Combine egg whites, salt, and 1 cup of powdered sugar in electric mixer until blended
  2. Add more powdered sugar and vanilla if desired (vanilla tints the icing into an off-white color)
  3. Continue adding powdered sugar until you have your desired consistency
  4. Icing should be thick like toothpaste for fine details and writing, and thin like maple syrup to decorate large areas
  5. Decorate cookie
  6. Let icing harden completely between layers (1 hour)
  7. Let finished cookie set for at least 4 hours before bagging/boxing
